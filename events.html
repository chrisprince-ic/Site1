Authentic Development Agency (ADA International) 

 (ADA International) is the development arm of the Authentic World Ministries with the goal of creating wealth and promoting people’s social-economic welfare.Over the past decade, ADA International leveraged the Africa Rise International Conference held annual in Kigali, Rwanda to organize Business to Business side events for collaborations, building partnerships and networking. Due to recent development in Rwanda with regards to the situation in the Eastern Democratic Republic of Congo, the Africa Rise International Conference with not be held in Rwanda. It’s in this context that Authentic Development Agency (ADA International) North America has organized an international conference to be held in Toronto, Ontario, Canada in August 2025 under the theme “Cultivating a conquering mindset".
